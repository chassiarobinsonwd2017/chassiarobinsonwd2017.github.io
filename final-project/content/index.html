INTRODUCTION

Benefits of Pilates

Elite trainers deliver an hour-long session that not only tones to produce a lean and strong silhouette, but also is great for rehabilitation, post-natal conditioning, weight loss, alignment and strength gain.

Our classes

Our classes are for everyone regardless of ability or size. Our expert trainers will ensure you have personal attention throughout your class to ensure you safely complete all movements and leave energised and invigorated. With over 250 movements, Pilates is the most varied and dynamic luxe fitness offering that produces significant results, focussing on flexibility, coordination and balance to leave you fitter, leaner, stronger.
